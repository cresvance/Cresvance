2026 Tax Update

Deductions: Sports, Pets & Global Assets

New regional tax benefits approved for 2026. Discover how to maximize your savings in your next tax return.

Read full article ↓

1. Andalusia Sports Deduction: You can now deduct 15% of your gym fees or sports-related expenses (up to €100). Essential for maintaining a healthy lifestyle while reducing your tax bill.


2. Veterinary Expenses: New for 2026 in Andalusia! You can recover a portion of the healthcare costs for your pets. Make sure to request official invoices.


3. Assets Abroad (Modelo 720): If you hold over €50,000 in foreign bank accounts or real estate, reporting is mandatory. We ensure your filing is correct to avoid heavy fines.
