Andalusia Fiscal Intelligence 2026

Maximize your Tax Returns

Living in Andalusia allows you to claim specific regional deductions. Our team ensures you recover every euro you are entitled to:

Sports and Health

You can recover 15% of your gym fees and sports club memberships. This is designed to promote a healthy lifestyle among residents. Limit of 100 euros annually.

Pet Healthcare

Andalusia is a pioneer in supporting pet owners. Claim 15% of your veterinary expenses for preventive treatments and check-ups for your pets.

Renting and Housing

Residents under 35 or over 65 years old can deduct 15% of their rent. Additionally, there are deductions for purchasing habitual residences in protected areas.

Family and Birth

Up to 200 euros per child for birth or adoption. This increases significantly in cases of international adoption or for residents in smaller municipalities.

Educational Expenses

Deduct costs related to language classes and textbooks for your children. We help you categorize these expenses to lower your final tax bill.

Disability Support

Dedicated tax credits for families supporting members with disabilities, ranging from 100 to 150 euros depending on the specific situation.
