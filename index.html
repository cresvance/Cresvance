2026 Tax Update

New Deductions: Sports, Gyms & Global Wealth

Spain is prioritizing health and transparency. From the new sports tax credits to mandatory global asset reporting, here is your 2026 guide.

Read full article ↓

1. The "Gym" Deduction is here: Great news for active taxpayers in the Spanish market!
• In Andalusia, a new deduction allows you to recover 15% of your gym or sports club fees (limit €100).
• In the Valencian Community, the benefit reaches 30% (limit €150).
This applies to the Modelo 100 filing. Remember to keep all your official invoices.


2. Modelo 720 & 721: If you hold assets abroad (bank accounts, real estate, or crypto) exceeding €50,000, reporting is mandatory. Penalties for missing deadlines are high, but we can help you file accurately.


3. RETA & Business Setup: Thinking of going freelance? Beyond RETA registration, we assist with the "Cuota Cero" (Zero Fee) applications in regions like Andalusia or Madrid for new entrepreneurs.
