Andalusia 2026

Exclusive Tax Deductions in Andalusia

The Junta de Andalucía has approved new tax credits for 2026. From sports activities to pet healthcare, find out how much you can recover.

Explore deductions ↓

1. Sports and Gym Deduction: Andalusian taxpayers can deduct 15% of expenses related to sports services (gyms, sports centers, and federated clubs) with a maximum limit of €100.


2. Veterinary Expenses: A major update for pet owners! You can now deduct 15% of veterinary costs, including medical check-ups and preventive treatments, with a limit of €50 per animal.


3. Renting Property: Deduct 15% of your annual rent (up to €600, or €900 for people with disabilities). This applies to tenants under 35 years old or over 65.


4. Work and Family Balance: Credits available for domestic help and childcare, alongside deductions for investment in protected or social housing.
