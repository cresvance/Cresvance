Digital Nomad Visa · Spain

Live & work in Spain. Legally.

Spain's Digital Nomad Visa is an amazing opportunity — but the paperwork can be confusing.

Our English-speaking legal team helps you relocate to Spain legally, smoothly, and with confidence — whether you apply from your home country's consulate or already from within Spain.

Who qualifies

Non-EU nationals working remotely for foreign companies or clients, earning at least 200% of Spain's minimum wage.

Document preparation

We guide you through every required document: contracts, apostilles, criminal records, health insurance and more.

Consulate or in-country

Apply from your home consulate before arriving, or switch status from inside Spain. We support both routes.

Beckham Law tax benefit

Pay a flat 24% income tax rate instead of up to 47% for the first 6 years. We handle the application for you.

Family reunification

Bring your spouse and dependant children. We manage the joint application to keep your family together.

Renewal support

Your visa lasts up to 3 years and can be renewed. We track your deadlines and handle all renewals proactively.
